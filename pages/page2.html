About

Welcome to the Saint Louis School of Aurora Hill, Inc.! The school is an effective instrument of integral evangelization leading communities to Christ through holistic school programs.











School Vision/Mission

As a Catholic Institution, Saint Louis School of Aurora Hill, Inc. envisions to mold Christ-centered learners who commit solemnly themselves in the service of others with utmost integrity and fully respect for cultural diversity through our excellent academic formation.

  • Setting the school as means of Catholic Evangelization.
  • Nurturing a culture of dialogue and collaboration.
  • Practicing participative and empowering school leadership.
  • Promoting excellent academic programs.
